ZeroChaos Online Enrollment


Welcome to ZeroChaos!

The ZeroChaos 'Online Enrollment' process is the first step in setting up your payroll account.Please click on the 'Next' button below, and then work your way through the pages until all the Required Fields are complete.

Information that you enter into each field will be automatically saved as you move from page to page, and as a page's Required Fields are completed, its bubble in the Progress Bar will get a checkmark and the % Completion gauge will be updated.

When all Required Fields are complete and the Progress Bar is missing no checkmarks, your status will advance to 'Pending Review', and you will be able to access the 'Sign Docs' page, but you will no longer be able to edit any Required Fields except on the 'Emergency Information' page.

If you need assistance, please contact the ZeroChaos OnBoarding Team at 877-937-6242, opt 1 or service@zerochaos.com.

Document Signing Session

The last step in the ZeroChaos Online enrollment process is the signing of required paperwork. Click on each document in the 'Pending Documents' panel to submit your electronic signature.
As you successfully complete and sign each document, it will be automatically moved from the 'Pending Documents' panel to the 'Completed Documents' panel.

ZeroChaos cannot process your payroll until your enrollment is complete. Enrollment is complete when all Required Fields are complete and there are no documents left in the 'Pending Documents' panel. When complete, the '% Completion' gauge will show '100%'.

You can access this page and your documents at any time in the future. Put a check in the checkbox of any document that you want to save and then click on the panel's 'Download Selected Documents' button.

Adobe Acrobat Reader is required in order to access the PDF files; it can be downloaded for free from the Adobe website: Download Adobe Reader

If you need any assistance, please contact the ZeroChaos OnBoarding Team at 1-877-937-6242, Option 2 or enrollments@zerochaos.com
