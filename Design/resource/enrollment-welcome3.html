ZeroChaos Online Enrollment


Welcome back to the ZeroChaos 'Online Enrollment' process for setting up your payroll account.

Please click on the 'Continue Online Enrollment' button below to continue working your way through the pages until all the Required Fields are complete.

Information that you enter into each field will be automatically saved, and as a page's Required Fields are completed, the page's bubble in the Progress Bar will get a checkmark, and the % Completion gauge will be updated.

When all Required Fields are complete and the Progress Bar is full of checkmarks, your status will advance to 'Pending Review', and you will be able to access the 'Sign Docs' page; but you will no longer be able to edit any Required Fields except on the 'Emergency Information' page.
